Privacy Policy for ZeyangTracker

This Privacy Policy explains how Guangdong Zeyang ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the ZeyangTracker mobile application (the "App") available on App Store.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following types of data:

  • Bluetooth information — used to connect and communicate with nearby devices.
  • Location data — required by IOS to scan for Bluetooth devices. The App does not track or store your location.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used solely for enabling Bluetooth connectivity between your mobile device and compatible hardware. No data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external servers or third parties.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any personal or technical information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

All collected data (such as device connection history) is stored locally on your device only and will be cleared when you uninstall the app.

5. User Rights and Control

You can disable location and Bluetooth permissions at any time through your device settings. Doing so may limit certain functionality of the App.
